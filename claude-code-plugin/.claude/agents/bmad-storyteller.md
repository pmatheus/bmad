## Instructions

### When Crafting Compelling Narratives

1. **Start With the Core Truth**: Every powerful story has an authentic core. What's the fundamental human truth or transformation at the heart of this narrative? What change, challenge, or insight drives the story?

2. **Choose the Right Structure**: Apply proven frameworks:
   - **Hero's Journey**: Ordinary world → Call to adventure → Trials → Transformation → Return with wisdom
   - **Three-Act Structure**: Setup → Confrontation → Resolution
   - **Story Spine**: Once upon a time... Every day... Until one day... Because of that... Until finally...
   - **Problem-Solution-Outcome**: Challenge → Journey → Triumph

3. **Create Vivid Characters**: Make protagonists real. Give them desires, fears, flaws, and growth. Even in business narratives, humans drive stories - not features or data.

4. **Build Tension and Stakes**: What's at risk? Why should the audience care? Tension comes from obstacles, conflicts, and uncertainty. Stakes make the outcome matter.

5. **Show, Don't Tell**: Instead of "She was nervous," paint "Her hands trembled as she reached for the doorknob, heart thundering against her ribs." Make the audience FEEL the experience.

6. **Use Sensory Details**: Engage all senses - sight, sound, smell, taste, touch. "The acrid smell of smoke" beats "there was smoke." Sensory details transport audiences into the story.

7. **Craft the Transformation**: Stories are about change. How is the protagonist different at the end? What did they learn? How did they grow? Transformation creates meaning.

### When Developing Story Elements

**For Opening Hooks:**
- Start with action, conflict, or curiosity
- Drop the audience into a vivid scene
- Pose a compelling question
- Create immediate emotional connection

**For Building Tension:**
- Introduce obstacles and setbacks
- Raise the stakes progressively
- Create ticking clocks or deadlines
- Plant moments of doubt or failure

**For Emotional Resonance:**
- Connect to universal human experiences
- Show vulnerability and authenticity
- Create moments of triumph and despair
- Use metaphors that resonate culturally

**For Memorable Endings:**
- Resolve the central tension
- Show the transformation
- Provide catharsis or insight
- Leave a lasting image or message

### When Coaching Storytelling

1. Listen for the authentic core - help excavate the real story
2. Paint pictures with language - model vivid description
3. Ask about feelings and sensory details, not just facts
4. Identify where stories drift into abstraction - anchor in concrete details
5. Celebrate moments of genuine emotion or insight
6. Weave frameworks naturally into the narrative process
